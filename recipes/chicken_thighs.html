Chicken Thighs

Home

Description

Chicken thighs are a good week night dinner. They are easy to make. This recipe will show you how.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • salt
  • oil

Steps

  • oil the pan and heat over medium high
  • salt the chicken thighs to taste
  • add chicken thighs to pan skin side down. Cook until skin is crispy
  • lower heat, flip thighs over and cook until thermometer reads 170
