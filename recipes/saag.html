Saag

Home

Description

Saag is an indian spinach dish. It has spices and is very tasty. you can make it at home by following this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Tumeric

Steps

  1. Start by dicing and pan frying the onion and garlic
  2. add spices. cook until fragrant
  3. add the tomatoes. cook for 10 minutes
  4. add the spinach. add some water, cook until spinach cooks down.
  5. use an immersion blender to blend up the spinach a bit
  6. add cream to taste
  7. serve with rice or naan
